User Privacy Policy

 

Effective Date: 31.08.2024

 

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our Embroidery:Stitch View&Convert  Android application.

 

1. Data Collection

 

We respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your personal data is protected. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information or data from users of our application.

The device storage is accessed only for the loading and saving of embroidery patterns for use in the application. No other files are accessed or modified. The embroidery pattern files are not being distributed or modified without the users action.

However, Embroidery:Stitch View&Convert includes advertising through integration with Google AdMob, which may collect certain information as described below.

2. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to serve ads within our application. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data as outlined in its own Privacy Policy. This may include information such as:

  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system)
  • Advertising ID
  • Location data (e.g., approximate location based on IP address)
  • Usage data (e.g., app interactions, ad interactions)

Google AdMob uses this data to provide targeted advertisements and to improve the ad experience.

We use Google AdMob messaging tool to meet the requirements of Google’s EU user consent policy and EU regulation when gathering consent from users for showing personalized or non-personalized ads. For more details on how Google AdMob collects and uses data, please review Google’s Privacy Policy https://policies.google.com/privacy and https://support.google.com/admob/answer/48182.

3. Third-Party Services

 

In addition to Google AdMob, we do not integrate with any other third-party services that collect user data.

4. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. However, since Google AdMob is used in our app, it is important to note that Google has its own policies regarding children’s data. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided personal information through Google AdMob, please review Google’s privacy practices and contact us or Google directly if you have concerns.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

 

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated version will be effective as of the date indicated at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
